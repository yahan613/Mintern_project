information about me


Benjamin Graham (1894-1976) was a renowned American economist, investor, and professor. He is widely regarded as one of the most influential figures in the world of finance and investing. Graham is often referred to as the "father of value investing". Graham's ideas and principles had a profound impact on the investment community and continue to influence investors, including legendary figures like Warren Buffett, who was one of his students.
